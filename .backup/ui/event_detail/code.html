The Pinnacle of Precision

Experience the raw energy and architectural precision of the Nordic Open. This isn't just a tournament; it's a curated gathering for those who appreciate the intersection of high-performance athletics and elite sport facility design.

Our 2024 edition features a specialized 32-bracket professional draw alongside an exclusive amateur invitational. Hosted within the award-winning glass walls of the Stockholm Padel Cathedral, spectators will enjoy unobstructed views of tactical mastery and explosive power.
